Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2024

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information about our users. We only store information that is essential for the functionality of the Kaede Bot API, including:

  • Bot Tokens: When you register a bot with our API, we generate a unique bot token for authentication purposes. This token is necessary for your bot to interact with the API.
  • Usernames: We store bot usernames for identification and management purposes.
  • Connection Status: We keep track of whether a bot is connected to the API to ensure proper command delivery.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving the Kaede Bot API. We use this information to:

  • Authenticate bots and manage their connections.
  • Deliver commands from the console to the appropriate bot.
  • Monitor the status and performance of the API.

Data Storage and Security

We understand the sensitivity of bot tokens and user data. To enhance security, we implement the following measures:

  • Encrypted Storage: Bot tokens are not stored in plain text within our database. They are stored in an encrypted format to protect them from unauthorized access.
  • Secure Server: Our API server is hosted on a secure platform with appropriate security measures in place.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to our server and database.

Discord Bot Token Storage

We want to explicitly state that we do NOT store your Discord bot token in our API database. Your Discord bot token is stored securely within the user files on your local system. It is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Data Retention

We retain bot tokens and related information for as long as the bot is registered with our API. If you choose to unregister your bot, this information will be deleted from our systems.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your information with any third-party services or organizations.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unregister Your Bot: You can unregister your bot at any time, which will delete your bot token and related information from our systems.
  • Contact Us: If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at cleaverdeath@gmail.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
